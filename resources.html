Cybersecurity Awareness Campaign at Peace Royal International School

On February 7, 2025, we had the incredible opportunity to host a Cybersecurity Awareness Campaign at Peace Royal International School, engaging with the Junior High School 2 class. This interactive session aimed to educate students on the importance of online safety, cyber hygiene, and responsible digital behavior. Through engaging discussions, real-world examples, and hands-on activities, students learned how to identify cyber threats, create strong passwords, avoid online scams, and protect their personal information. The session also emphasized the importance of being cyber savvy, cyber smart, and cyber safe in today's digital world. We are proud to have inspired the next generation to navigate the internet securely and responsibly. This campaign is just one step in our ongoing mission to promote cybersecurity awareness among young learners. 🚀🔒
